Know more about us. We’re on a mission to share the taste of Nigeria

We’re on a mission to share the taste of Nigeria—one store, one kitchen, one table at a time. We’re more than a cultural grocery store we’re your gateway to authentic African and flavors in Canada. From hard-to-find yam tubers and egusi seeds to premium palm oil and ready-to-cook frozen fish, our hand-selected products bring the taste of home to your kitchen.
