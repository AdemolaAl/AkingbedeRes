Akhigbe Catering: Authentic African food for Your Next Event

As a proud division of Akhigbe African Food Market Inc., our catering arm is led by Rita a master Nigerian caterer with over 25 years crafting unforgettable meals in the restaurant world

Catering Insights

Led by Rita, our celebrated Nigerian chef with 25+ years of culinary mastery, Akhigbe Catering brings authentic Nigerian flavors straight to your event. From aromatic jollof rice and sizzling suya platters to comforting pepper soup and crispy plantain bites, every dish is prepared fresh with premium ingredients. Whether it’s weddings, birthdays, corporate events, or private dinners, we deliver vibrant meals that keep guests coming back for more. Let us turn your gathering into a feast to remember tailored menus, timely delivery, and unforgettable taste guaranteed

Our Location

You can visit us at 236 Sherbourne Street, Toronto, conveniently located in the heart of the city. Our venue is easily accessible by public transit and offers ample parking for guests. Stop by to experience our welcoming atmosphere and see where we prepare authentic African cuisine for your special events.
